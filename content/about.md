**Daily News Digest** is an automated news briefing covering artificial
intelligence, cloud computing, and developer tools.

Every morning it pulls fresh stories from a curated set of RSS feeds, hands them
to [Claude Code](https://www.claude.com/product/claude-code) to read, filter, and
synthesize, and publishes a single scannable digest of the day's most significant
news — grouped by theme and ranked by relevance.

## Why?

The AI and developer-tools space moves fast, and keeping up means juggling a
dozen newsletters, blogs, and changelogs. This site collapses all of that into
one short read per day: skip the marketing fluff, group the duplicate coverage,
and surface what actually matters.

## What you'll find

- **One digest per day**, published automatically at 5 AM EST.
- **Theme-based sections** (frontier models, security, policy, developer tools,
  and more) that adapt to whatever the day's news actually contains.
- **Relevance tiers** — the biggest stories get a full summary; smaller items get
  a line or a link.
- A complete [list of sources](/sources/) and a [breakdown of how it all
  works](/how-it-works/).

## Open source

The entire pipeline — feed configuration, aggregator, and site — is open source.
Browse the code or suggest a feed on
[GitHub](https://github.com/dmorand17/news-aggregator).
