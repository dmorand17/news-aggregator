> **New York Becomes the First State to Enact a Data Center Moratorium**
> *The Verge / TechCrunch*
> Governor Kathy Hochul signed an executive order blocking new environmental permits for hyperscale data centers for up to a year — the nation's first statewide action of its kind. The moratorium cites surging electricity costs, water consumption, and erosion of local control as AI infrastructure investment accelerates. A separate legislative bill that could impose even broader restrictions still awaits her signature, potentially setting New York as the template for how states push back on the AI buildout.
