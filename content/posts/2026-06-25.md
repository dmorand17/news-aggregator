> **[OpenAI and Broadcom Unveil Jalapeño Inference Chip](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/24/openai-and-broadcom-reveal-jalapeno-first-ai-chip-in-partnership.html)** — *CNBC*

Built for gigawatt-scale data center deployments, the chip signals OpenAI's ambition to own the entire stack — compute, model, and product — rather than remain dependent on Nvidia.
