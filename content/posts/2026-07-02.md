> **Fable 5 Is Back — With New Safeguards**
> [Redeploying Fable 5](https://www.anthropic.com/news/redeploying-fable-5) — *Anthropic*

To redeploy, Anthropic added a new classifier blocking the reported jailbreak technique in over 99% of cases, rerouting flagged requests to Claude Opus 4.8. Fable 5 is available on Claude.ai, Claude Code, and the Claude Platform for Pro/Max/Team users up to 50% of weekly limits through July 7, then via usage credits; Mythos 5 remains restricted to approved US organizations under the Glasswing program.
