> **GPT-5.6 Family Goes Public After 12-Day Government Preview**
> *OpenAI* — OpenAI today publicly released GPT-5.6 Sol (flagship), Terra (balanced), and Luna (fast) across ChatGPT, the API, and Codex, ending a government-requested access restriction in place since June 26. The White House's ONCD and OSTP had asked OpenAI to limit the launch to ~20 vetted organizations citing Sol's advanced cybersecurity capabilities. Alongside the models, OpenAI debuted ChatGPT Work — an agent that gathers context across connected apps to produce documents, spreadsheets, and presentations — rolling out first to Mac and Windows desktop.

**[Anthropic's GRAM: Removable Dual-Use Knowledge Compartments](https://www.anthropic.com/research/off-switch-dual-use)** — *Anthropic*

Gradient-Routed Auxiliary Modules (GRAM) give models dedicated compartments for dual-use knowledge that are only updated when learning from dual-use data and can be deleted entirely after training.
