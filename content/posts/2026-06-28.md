> **ChatGPT Logs as Courtroom Evidence — A Legal First**
> Prosecutors in the federal arson trial of Jonathan Rinderknecht — accused of sparking the 2025 Palisades wildfire that killed dozens — introduced his ChatGPT conversation history as evidence, showing he generated AI images of the Pacific Palisades burning and researched fire liability hours before the blaze. Despite the landmark use of AI logs, the trial ended in a mistrial Friday after jurors deadlocked 10-2 for acquittal. Rinderknecht faces retrial in the fall, and the case sets a precedent for AI data in criminal prosecutions.
