Anthropic's most dangerous AI model just fell into the wrong hands The Verge

Anthropic's Mythos AI model — a powerful cybersecurity tool the company itself warned could be dangerous — was accessed by a "small group of unauthorized users," according to Bloomberg. A member of the group identified as a third-party contractor reportedly shared access via a private online forum. Anthropic says it is investigating but claims no systems were impacted, a response that may not satisfy regulators or enterprise customers given Mythos's offensive security capabilities.

Mozilla: Anthropic's Mythos found 271 security vulnerabilities in Firefox 150 Ars Technica

Using Anthropic's Mythos AI, Mozilla identified and patched 271 zero-day security vulnerabilities in Firefox 150 — a scale that would have taken months longer with traditional fuzzing or even elite human researchers. The result powerfully illustrates both the defensive potential and the serious risks of AI-powered security tooling, providing stark context for the Mythos unauthorized access incident reported the same day.
